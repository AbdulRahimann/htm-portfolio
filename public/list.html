Angela's Recipie for the best cinnamon rolls

Ingredients

for the dough

  • half cup of warm milk
  • teaspon of yeast
  • half cup of sugar
  • half cup of butter
  • one egg
  • two and a half cups of flour

for the filling

  • half cup of brown sugar
  • table spoon of cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

instructions

  1. mix the milk with the yeast and let it sit for 5 minutes
  2. add the sugar, butter, egg, and flour to the mixture
  3. knead the dough until it is smooth and elastic
  4. let the dough rise for 1 hour
  5. roll out the dough and spread the filling evenly
  6. roll the dough into a log and cut into slices
  7. place the slices on a baking sheet and let them rise for 30 minutes
  8. bake at 375°F for 20-25 minutes
